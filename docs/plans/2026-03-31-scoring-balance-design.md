# Scoring Balance + Draft Redesign — Design

**Date:** 2026-03-31
**Status:** Approved

## Problem

Game scoring averages are significantly higher than intended. Real NBA games in 2025 range from ~96 to ~152 per team. The current game engine regularly produces 200+ point games. The mean needs to come down while preserving the variance that makes games exciting.

## Root Causes

1. **Base scoring charts** are generous — even bad rolls produce decent points (can't change these, baked into physical cards)
2. **Shot check cards stack too easily** — a single section can produce many bonus shot checks on top of 10 scoring rolls
3. **Assist/rebound costs are too cheap** — 4-5 assists converts into a scoring explosion via cheap 3PT checks

## Constraints

- Cannot modify the 306 player scoring charts (physical cards exist)
- Matchup advantages should remain exploitable — that's the core strategic identity
- Changes must feel natural, not artificial caps

## Design

### 1. Assist Economy Rebalance

| Spend | Current Cost | New Cost | Rationale |
|-------|-------------|----------|-----------|
| +1 to shot check | 1 AST | 1 AST (unchanged) | Weak option, good bait — teaches resource management |
| Paint shot check | 2 AST | **3 AST** | 2pts is valuable, should cost more |
| 3PT shot check | 2 AST | **4 AST** | 3pts is the most valuable check, needs to be a real investment. 4 AST with Steph was too easy at 2. |
| 5 AST bonus draw | 5 AST | 5 AST (unchanged) | |

### 2. Rebound Economy Rebalance

| Bonus | Current | New | Rationale |
|-------|---------|-----|-----------|
| Paint check | +3 diff, costs 2 REB | +3 diff, **costs 3 REB** | Spend what you earn — feels fair |
| Fast break | +5 diff, costs 3 REB | **REMOVED** | Speed÷4 is clunky mental math for tabletop. Confusing mechanic. |
| Putback | 2+ individual REB, costs 2 REB | Unchanged | Good mechanic, appropriately priced |
| Win rebound track | +1 stored assist | Unchanged | |

### 3. Draft Phase Redesign

**Current:** 10-pick snake draft (A→B→B→A→A→B→B→A→A→B) with visible alternating picks.

**Problem:** First picker is disadvantaged — opponent can counter-matchup your visible star pick with their best defender. Encourages hiding your best player, which is unintuitive for new players.

**New: Simultaneous Blind Selection**

- Both teams see their 10-player roster with fatigue/hot/cold info
- Each team privately selects 5 starters (click to toggle selection)
- "Submit" button locks picks
- **Solo mode:** Player submits, AI picks revealed simultaneously
- **PvP mode:** Both players submit independently, lineups reveal once both are locked (same mutual confirmation pattern as end-section votes)
- **Tutorial:** Guided selection with tooltips, AI picks on submit

After reveal → proceed directly to Matchup Strategy Phase.

**Strategic impact:** You must predict your opponent's lineup without seeing it. Do you play your star knowing they might rest theirs? Do you stack offense or balance with defenders? This adds a poker-like read element.

### 4. Remove Fast Break Mechanic

Remove entirely from:
- `spendReboundBonus()` in engine.js — remove `fast_break` case
- `endSection()` — remove fast break from rebound bonus tracking
- UI — remove fast break spend buttons
- Rulebook/How to Play content
- Analytics tracking

### 5. Analytics (Already Implemented)

Collapsible analytics panel tracks all scoring sources:
- Chart points vs shot check points vs assist-spend points vs rebound bonus points
- Shot check hit rates
- Cards played
- Assists/rebounds generated by source

This will validate whether the economy changes bring scoring into the target range.

## Expected Impact

- **Assist nerf (2→4 for 3PT):** Roughly halves the number of bonus 3PT checks per game. At ~3pts each, this could reduce scoring by 10-20 pts per team per game.
- **Rebound nerf (2→3 for paint, fast break removed):** Reduces bonus paint checks and eliminates fast break entirely. ~5-10 pts per team per game reduction.
- **Draft redesign:** Indirect scoring impact — harder to engineer perfect matchups when you can't see opponent picks. Reduces average roll bonuses slightly.
- **Combined estimate:** 15-30 point reduction per team per game, bringing the mean from ~200+ down toward 150-170 range. Further tuning possible with new defensive cards in a future pass.
